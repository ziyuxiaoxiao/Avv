今日精句：
I'll have to do those letters tomorrow-I'm a bit pressed for 
time this afternoon.
我明天才能处理这些信件，今天下午我时间有点紧。
词汇解析：
pressed 时间、金钱、空间等紧张
【例】This will relieve pressure on the trains to some extent.
这将在某种程度上减轻对火车的压力。
【例】The landlord is putting pressure on us to move out.
房东强迫我们搬走。
【例】If the management won't listen,we'll have to bring some more
pressure to bear.
如果管理层对我们的话充耳不闻，我们就得施加更大的压力。
put pressure on sb.(to do sth.)
bring pressure to bear(on sb.)(to do sth.)
向某人施加压力（要其做某事）、强迫、迫使
【例】I'll have to do those letters tomorrow-I'm a bit pressed for time
this afternoon.
be pressed/pushed for money,space,time,etc.
金钱（或空间、时间等）有限
